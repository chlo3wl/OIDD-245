About This Project

Chloe Lin's data project for OIDD 2450 - Analytics and the Digital Economy

I have always been passionate about music being a universal experience that transcends language, culture, and geography. Music festivals is a unique experience that brings people from all walks of life come togeher to celebrate their love for music and create memories that will last a lifetime. From classical to jazz, from hip hop to metal, every genre has something to offer!

This project aims to provide insights into the global music festival scene for the entire year of 2023. The comprehensive overview of this year's events will include details on festival location, lineup, tickets, etc. The analyses will uncover interest trends that can help festivalgoers incorporate a stop at a music festival on their next vacation and unlock new experiences.

Global Spotlight

Immerse yourself in different cultures and genres

TLDR: Europe hosts the highest number of music festivals, with electronic and rock music being the most popular genres. Conversely, in the US, rock music reigns supreme followed by electronic music. The best time to attend festivals is generally during the summer. London, Las Vegas, and Paris are among the most popular festival cities. Choose the complementary experience that best suits your desires to enhance your festival experience.

U.S. Spotlight

Find your next festival destination

TLDR: US music festivals are a haven for music lovers, with concentrated clusters of festivals in coastal cities. Each festival generally caters to particular music genres. Hangout Festival, Governors Ball, and Coachella being some of the biggest ones hosting top artists like Bad Bunny. The fans of Lil Nas X, Florence + the Machine, and Billie Eilish are so lucky that their beloved artist frequents festivals.
